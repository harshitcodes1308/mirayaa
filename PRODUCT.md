# Product

## Register

brand

## Users
Mirayaa serves mobile-first jewelry shoppers in India who want handcrafted oxidised silver and fashion jewelry that feels culturally rooted, feminine, and affordable. Buyers are browsing quickly on phones, comparing style, price, delivery trust, and whether a piece feels special enough to wear or gift.

## Product Purpose
Mirayaa.in replaces the current Wix store with a premium, SEO-friendly e-commerce experience for handcrafted Indian jewelry. It must sell accessible luxury pieces from Rs. 99 to Rs. 249, make the two core collections easy to explore, support cart and checkout with Razorpay, and give the owner a practical admin surface for products, categories, and orders.

## Brand Personality
Tarnished, intimate, luminous. The brand should feel like oxidised metal catching warm light: handcrafted, culturally rich, and precise rather than mass-produced or template-pretty.

## Anti-references
No generic AI gradient cards, floating blobs, purple-blue tech palettes, stock beige luxury, fake urgency timers, green oval cart buttons, 3D mockups, lorem ipsum, or bland copy such as "Discover our amazing collection." The site should not look like a Shopify theme with jewelry swapped in.

## Design Principles
- Let product imagery and materiality carry the brand before decoration does.
- Keep the interface sharp and editorial: low radii, thin rules, clear hierarchy, no soft template cards.
- Use gold as a glint, not a blanket; the dark oxidised base stays in charge.
- Build for thumb-first shopping on Indian mobile networks, with fast scanning and stable layouts.
- Make craft feel accessible: premium presentation, honest prices, no artificial scarcity.

## Accessibility & Inclusion
Target WCAG AA contrast for text and controls. Respect reduced-motion preferences by replacing movement-heavy reveals with opacity changes or instant states. Keep touch targets large on mobile, use semantic controls, visible focus states, and numeric keyboards for phone and pincode fields.
